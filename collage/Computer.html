Courses Offered

Computer Engineering

Vision

To equip the students with adequate technical knowledge for inserting them into real-time projects and to prepare them to contribute their expertise in the field of academics, industries, and society.

Mission

M1: To produce best quality computer science diploma graduates and make them comprehend basic concepts in computer engineering through intellectual inputs.

M2: To impart quality education, hands-on experience, and value education to be sensitive to the environment, safety, and economic context in solving real-world problems.

M3: To expose our students to intensive training in computer engineering tools and applications and to prepare the students for the industry and professional career.

Admission Criteria

Loyola Polytechnic (YSRR), Pulivendla is a Christian Minority Institution which is primarily (30% of seats) meant for Christian students. It is also open to others for the rest of the seats without distinction of caste, creed, or religion according to Government rules in force.

Diploma in Computer Engineering - Intake 120 Seats
Duration of Course - 3 Years

Eligibility Criteria

Selection of candidates is governed by the rules and regulations laid down in this regard from time to time. Candidates who wish to seek admission in any of the diploma courses will have to appear for a common entrance examination (POLYCET) conducted by the State Board of Technical Education and Training, Andhra Pradesh, Hyderabad.

Documents Required at the Time of Admission

  • Marks card of S.S.C
  • Transfer Certificate (at the time of admission only)
  • Conduct Certificate
  • Study Certificate IV to X
  • Residence Certificate
  • Physical Fitness Certificate
  • Caste Certificate if belongs to SC/ST and BC
  • Income Certificate
