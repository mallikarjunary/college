Courses Offered

Mechanical Engineering

Vision

To dispense pre-eminence education and to enhance technical skills of the students pertaining to knowledge in Mechanical Engineering with the basis to mold the students for Industrial needs and to motivate them to be innovative and ethical as well.

Mission

M1: To produce best quality civil engineering diploma graduates and make them comprehend basic concepts in civil engineering through intellectual inputs.

M2: To impart quality education, hands-on experience, and value education to be sensitive to the environment, safety, and economic context in solving real-world problems.

M3: To expose our students to intensive training in civil engineering tools and applications and to prepare the students for the industry and professional career.

Admission Criteria

Loyola Polytechnic (YSRR), Pulivendla is a Christian Minority Institution which is primarily (30% of seats) meant for Christian students. As per the present admission policy, 30% of the seats are under minority quota and the rest of the seats will be filled by the Government as per the norms of reservations in force. The seats under minority quota will be filled by merit in the qualifying examination.
